## Selected Research

### Model Collapse and Generalization

I study model collapse through the lens of generalization degradation in diffusion models. Our work connects model generalization with the entropy of training-data distributions and develops entropy-aware data selection and curriculum priors that mitigate collapse while preserving sample fidelity.

### UAV Object Detection via Diffusion-based Domain Adaptation

We proposed a hierarchical Sim2Real diffusion framework with coarse-to-fine noise alignment for synthetic and real UAV imagery. The method improved mAP by **7.1%** on VisDrone compared with standard YOLOv8 baselines.
