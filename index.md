## About

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the **University of Michigan**, advised by [Prof. Qing Qu](https://qingqu.engin.umich.edu/). I am currently completing my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, focusing on signal processing, image processing, and machine learning.
